Business Purpose
----------------------------------------
Aptness.ai provides a chat-powered automation platform to connect apps, automate tasks, and streamline operations for businesses.

Detected Integrations
----------------------------------------
  • [EMAIL] Gmail Integration: Integrates with Gmail to send and receive emails, trigger workflows based on new emails, and automate email-related tasks. (auth: OAuth 2.0)
  • [CRM] Hubspot CRM Integration: Integrates with Hubspot CRM to manage contacts, create deals, and automate marketing and sales workflows. (auth: API Key)
  • [CLOUD STORAGE] Google Drive Integration: Integrates with Google Drive to upload files, manage folders, and automate file-related tasks. (auth: OAuth 2.0)

Automation Pipelines
----------------------------------------
  • New Email to CRM Contact (trigger: New email received in Gmail)
    Steps: Extract sender information from the email → Search for the contact in Hubspot CRM → If the contact exists, update the contact's information → If the contact does not exist, create a new contact in Hubspot CRM
  • Schedule Event and Send Email (trigger: User command via chat to schedule an event)
    Steps: Parse event details (date, time, description) from user input → Create event in Google Calendar → Send a confirmation email to the attendees via Gmail
