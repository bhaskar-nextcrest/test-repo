### navigate_to_section(section_name: string)
Navigates the agent to a specific section on the Aptness.ai homepage by scrolling to the relevant heading. Valid sections include "Marketplace", "Custom Agents", "Rapid Agent", "How it works", "Share your magic", "Everything in the box".

### describe_task_to_rapid_agent(task_description: string)
Fills the text area on the homepage designed for the 'Rapid Agent' feature with a specified task description. This simulates expressing a task to the AI for execution.

### list_integrated_apps()
Retrieves a list of applications that Aptness integrates with, as displayed in the 'Connect your apps' section of the homepage.

### start_getting_started_flow()
Clicks the 'Get started free' button typically found in the header, initiating the user onboarding or signup process.
